## Overview

LevelShift delivers across **six interconnected practice areas**, all increasingly AI-powered. The overarching commercial offer is enterprise AI transformation — the other five practices are both delivery vehicles and AI entry points.

## Practice 1 — Enterprise AI Transformation (Flagship)

The primary commercial identity of LevelShift post-rebrand. Uses a proprietary methodology to take enterprises from AI awareness to AI-native operation.

### 5-Phase Value Realization Framework

| Phase | Name | What Happens |
|-------|------|--------------|
| 1 | Strategic Clarity | Define AI objectives, investment boundaries, and success criteria with executive leadership |
| 2 | Functional AI Discovery | Map every business function and subprocess to identify where AI creates measurable value |
| 3 | Quantification | Score and prioritise each AI opportunity by business impact and implementation feasibility |
| 4 | Architecture & Enablement | Design data foundation, integration layer, security model, and AI governance framework |
| 5 | Controlled Execution | Deploy with discipline, establish monitoring, measure outcomes, refine continuously |

### AI Center of Excellence Assets

- **150+ pre-built AI agents** — inherited from real enterprise engagements, not demos
- Proven **AI governance playbooks** and architecture patterns
- **AI Readiness Assessment** and **AI Maturity Assessment** diagnostic tools
- **AI Discovery & Assessment Workshops** for enterprise leadership teams
- Industry-specific AI libraries: Manufacturing, Financial Services, Technology
- **Agentforce Ready Partner** — autonomous agent deployment on Salesforce platform
