## Anchor Case Studies (Most Referenced in Sales & Marketing)

### SPINX — Microsoft Fabric (Retail)
**The challenge:** SPINX operated across multiple cloud databases (Oracle, SQL Server, Azure) with no unified view of retail operations.

**LevelShift's work:** Implemented Microsoft Fabric as the unified data layer, connecting and harmonising all data sources into a single, real-time retail intelligence platform.

**Why it matters:** This is a **Microsoft-corroborated** case study — validated by Microsoft, not just self-reported. It is LevelShift's primary reference for Microsoft Fabric capability and the retail vertical.

**Use when:** Talking to retail clients, pitching Microsoft Fabric, or proving data modernisation credentials.

---

### CITGO — Enterprise Data Platform (Energy)
**The challenge:** CITGO needed a centralised enterprise data platform to replace fragmented data silos across the organisation.

**LevelShift's work:** Deployed Microsoft Fabric OneLake as a zero-copy data platform with Azure Synapse integration, enabling centralised governance and cross-organisational data access.

**Why it matters:** Validates LevelShift's Fabric expertise in the energy sector. Demonstrates governance and compliance capability.

**Use when:** Talking to energy or industrial clients, or proving Fabric implementation at scale alongside SPINX.

---

### Ongweoweh Corp — D365 (Manufacturing)
**The challenge:** On-time delivery performance was inconsistent; operations lacked real-time visibility.

**LevelShift's work:** Implemented Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations to unify supply chain, production, and delivery data.

**Result:** 20% increase in on-time delivery — a hard operational metric.

**Use when:** Manufacturing prospects, D365 F&O conversations, ERP ROI discussions.
